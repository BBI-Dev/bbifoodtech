Mini Doughnut Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 100g butter
  • ⅓ cup caster sugar, plus extra to dust
  • ¾ cup plain flour
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ¼ cup natural yogurt
  • 1 medium free-range egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Method:

  1. Heat the oven to 200°C/180°C fan/gas 6. Melt the butter in the microwave and use a pastry brush to grease all the holes of the muffin tray with a little of the butter.
  2. Put the caster sugar, plain flour and bicarbonate of soda in a large mixing bowl and combine.
  3. In a small jug, mix the remaining melted butter with the yogurt, egg and vanilla. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently fold them together using a large metal spoon until just incorporated.
  4. Divide the mixture equally among the holes in the muffin tray using a teaspoon, then bake for 7-8 minutes or until golden brown and risen.
  5. Remove the muffin tray from the oven and roll the hot doughnut muffins in caster sugar.
