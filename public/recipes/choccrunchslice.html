Chocolate Cruch Slice

Ingredients:

  • 90g butter
  • ½ cup cornflakes
  • ½ cup desiccated coconut
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ½ cup flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder

Icing

  • ¾ cup icing sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1-2 tsp hot water

Method:

  1. Place butter in a microwave safe bowl and microwave for 30 seconds or until melted
  2. In a large mixing bowl put the cornflakes, coconut and sugar.
  3. Sift in flour, baking powder and cocoa. Mix together.
  4. Add in melted butter. Mix well
  5. Firmly press into a lined dish
  6. Microwave on medium high for 2 ½ to 3 minutes.
  7. Leave to cool and while the slice is cooling, make the icing.
  8. Ice while warm and leave to set.
  9. Cut into slices.

Filling ideas

  • apple and cinnamon
  • peach and passion fruit
  • pear and berries
  • mixed berries
  • savoury - spaghetti and cheese
