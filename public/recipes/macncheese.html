Mac and Cheese

The specialist technological knowledge I will learn when making this recipe is:

  • How to learn a basic recipe that can be adapted to suit taste preferences
  • To learn how to measure accurately
  • How to make a simple roux

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups macaroni
  • 2 T butter
  • 2 T plain flour
  • 200 ml of milk
  • 1-2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 100g tasty cheese, grated

Method:

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to boil and cook pasta following packet directions
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a medium saucepan
  3. One butter is melted add flour, while constantly whisking
  4. Gradually (slowly) add milk and continue whisking to prevent lumps
  5. Once all the milk is added, reduce heat and simmer for 3-4 minutes or until thick
  6. Add mustard and adjust seasoning and add most of the cheese Then remove from the heat
  7. Strain pasta and add to the sauce. Put in a small ovenproof dish, sprinkle with cheese and grill till golden. (optional) This can be modified in many ways. Discuss with your group what you would do ??

Notes

This is a great way to use up leftover roast chicken. If you don't have a sandwich press you can cook them for a few minutes on each side in a frying pan.
