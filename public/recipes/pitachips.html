Pita Chips

Ingredients

for 2 servings

  • 1 pita bread
  • 2 tablespoons (Tbsp) butter, margarine or oil

Optional extras:

  • 1 teaspoon(tsp) minced garlic or ¼ teaspoon (tsp) garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon cheese
  • Fresh or dried herbs such as parsley

Preparation

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C, fan bake.
  2. In a small bowl combine butter, garlic, cheese and herbs (if using).
  3. Spread the butter mixture over each pita.
  4. Cut the pita into eighths and spread chips out on a baking paper-lined baking sheet.
  5. If you want to make thinner chips, cut the wedges in half.
  6. Bake for approx 15 minutes until golden and crispy. Check halfway through and rotate the tray if needed.
  7. Allow to cool.
  8. Enjoy!
