Pikelets

The specialist technological knowledge I will learn when making this recipe is:

  • How to safely use a frypan
  • Measure correctly
  • Why we sift ingredients

Equipment:

  • Measuring cup
  • Sieve
  • Spoon
  • Whisk
  • Frypan
  • Bowls
  • Fish slice

Ingredients

  • 1 C flour
  • 1t baking powder
  • ¼ t salt
  • 1 T butter
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ C sugar
  • ¼ C milk

Method

  1. Sift flour, baking powder and salt into a bowl
  2. In another bowl beat egg and sugar until thick
  3. Add milk to the egg mix, mix gently with a whisk
  4. Add milk and egg mix into flour, mix carefully until combined
  5. Heat pan and butter to melt.
  6. Add a spoonful of mixture into the pan using the whole space.
  7. Cover until serving or place in the warming drawer
    Traditionally Served with whipped jam or cream...bon appetit
