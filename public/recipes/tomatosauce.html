Basic Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ brown onion, diced
  • 11-2 teaspoons crushed garlic
  • 1 tin (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil, oregano or mixed herbs
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste

Method:

  1. Gently fry the onion until translucent and add the garlic.
  2. Cook for 1 minute and add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste and basil.
  3. Bring to boil and add balsamic vinegar and brown sugar. Optional: add in olives or chilli
  4. Reduce the heat and simmer for approx 10-15 minutes until the sauce has thickened.
  5. Taste and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss through cooked pasta and serve with parmesan cheese and freshly chopped basil or parsley. Alternatively, store for later use.

Filling ideas

  • apple and cinnamon
  • peach and passion fruit
  • pear and berries
  • mixed berries
  • savoury - spaghetti and cheese
