White Chocolate Fudge

Ingredients

  • 250 grams White Chocolate
  • ½ can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 25 grams butter, diced

  • To decorate: Easter Eggs, smarties, sprinkles etc

Instructions

  1. Prepare a small slice tin or tray with baking paper, spray a little cooking spray on the tin to help the paper stick, set aside
  2. In a large microwave proof bowl add the chocolate broken into chunks, the condensed milk and the diced butter
  3. Microwave on medium heat (approx 500-600 watts)for 1 minute and stir
  4. Microwave for a further minute on medium heat and stir really well, combining all the ingredients
  5. If lumps of chocolate remain microwave for 20 second bursts until no lumps remain and all the ingredients are fully combined
  6. Add some smarties or other decorations, stir to combine
  7. Pour into the prepared slice tin and smooth to spread
  8. Add the remaining decorations to the top and chill in the refrigerator for 6 hours, or overnight
  9. Once set, slice into small squares and serve!
  10. You can store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks
  11. Enjoy!
