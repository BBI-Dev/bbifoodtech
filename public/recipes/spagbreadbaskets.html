Spaghetti Bread Baskets

  • Makes 12
  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 slices sandwich or toast bread
  • 50g melted butter or margarine
  • Approx ¾ cup spaghetti or baked beans
  • 1 cup grated cheese

Method

  1. Trim the crusts from the bread and brush one side of each slice with a little melted butter.
  2. Push the bread slices, buttered side down, into 12 muffin tins.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 10-15 minutes until crispy. At this stage the cases can be cooked and kept in an airtight container.
  4. Fill the cases evenly with a spoonful of spaghetti or baked beans. Top each with grated cheese.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 10-15 minutes until the cheese has melted.

Tips

Add to it!

  1. Add diced ham, salami or cooked bacon for extra flavour.

Or Swap it out!

  1. swap spaghetti for baked beans, creamed corn or eggs
